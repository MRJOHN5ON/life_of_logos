Angel's Empire

Cam Studio · Siberia, Russia

Ad campaign · Center-Webcam.pro

The campaign

I created visually bold banner variations for Angel's Empire, a webcam studio in Russia, to be featured on Center-Webcam.pro—a directory where models search for studios.

At the client's request, I incorporated the original "AE" angel wings logo, a signature brand element designed by a previous graphic designer, to ensure brand consistency. The design emphasizes what matters most to models—payouts from 50% to 90% and private, spacious rooms—using large, high-contrast typography for immediate visibility.

The style combines retro-futurism and glamour, drawing attention while reinforcing the studio's unique identity. The banner is optimized for digital platforms and tailored to appeal directly to models browsing studio listings.
