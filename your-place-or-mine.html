The brief

Kim Sheridan runs Injectables by Kim — an on-call injectables practice in Texas (Botox, filler, PRP microneedling). Treatments at the client’s home or hers: personal, high-end, not clinical cold.

She wanted the flirt line “Your place or mine?” in the mark, syringes that feel elegant (not scary), soft pink, and rose-gold accents for concierge glamour.

What shipped

  • Primary logo — script tagline + wand-style syringe + stars
  • Two business card layouts (personal + full-service back)
  • Canva files set up for print · FedEx Office print & ship
  • Rose + rose-gold palette for future marketing

Personal front

Service back

Kim Sheridan, CLT · 30+ years experience · full Your place or mine? mark with Injectables by Kim · Botox, Juvéderm, Voluma, PRP microneedling · Master Injector Specialist — plus soft star texture and a subtle profile illustration for editorial glam.

Exploration

Two earlier directions Kim didn’t choose — still part of the process before we landed on the star + wand mark above.

Direction A · editorial portrait

Fashion-illustration energy — glam face, pink glove + syringe, My Place / Or Yours script over oversized injectables type. Bold and memorable, but busy for a small card and harder to reproduce cleanly at print size.

Direction B · door & pin

Rose-gold syringe with an open door and map pin linked by a path — literal “my place or yours” mobile service. Strong concept; she preferred the wand + Your place or mine? lockup for the final brand instead.
