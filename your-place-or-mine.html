Your Place or Mine?

Boutique injectables • Logo & business card

The collaboration

I collaborated with "Your Place or Mine?" Injectables, a Texas-based on-call Botox boutique service delivering luxurious in-home treatments, to develop a flirty yet upscale logo and business card design inspired by owner Kim's concise vision of mobile beauty enhancement. At her request, I incorporated ethereal syringe motifs stylized as feminine wands with subtle starry accents, elegant script typography for the tagline "Your place or mine?", and a soft rose-pink palette accented in rose gold to evoke approachable glamour and concierge exclusivity in the aesthetics sector.
