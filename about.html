About Me

I’m Ryley Johnson. By day I work full time at Prophet Town LLC, a small dev studio supporting Department of War contracts and private tech. Life of Logos is my hobby—where I get to do the creative stuff I love.

I’m a retired firefighter who moved into tech (QA bootcamp at TripleTen, internships, and now dev-adjacent work). I’ve always been drawn to troubleshooting and making things look good. That combo led me to logo design, business cards, and Squarespace websites—and to building this portfolio so clients can see the range of work I do.

When I’m not designing or testing, I’m exploring Gen AI—Midjourney, Grok, Stable Diffusion, Comfy UI—for fun and for client concepts. I vibe-code sites, sketch ideas, and turn brand ideas into something people can actually use and hold in their hands.
