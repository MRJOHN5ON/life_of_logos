The project

These colorful, bilingual business cards were created for Cory, a rideshare driver who spent a decade in Colombia and proudly speaks Spanish. The illustrated Honda features bold stripes reminiscent of the Colombian flag, highlighting Cory’s personal story and the car he drives.

A clean teal-and-black layout showcases easy-to-read contact info, along with Spanish messaging (¡Yo hablo español!) to welcome both English- and Spanish-speaking riders. QR codes for Venmo and Cash App provide a convenient touch, making these cards both visually engaging and practical for on-the-go customers.
